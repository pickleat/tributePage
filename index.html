Diego Valeri is an Argentinian soccer player for the Portland Timbers of MLS.

Personal Information

  • Full Name: Diego Hernan Valeri
  • Date of Birth: May 1, 1986
  • Place of Birth: Lanus, Buenos Aires, Argentina
  • Position: Central Attacking Midfield, Wide-Foward, Withdrawn Striker

Clubs

  • Lanus (Argentina) 2003-2013
  • Portland Timbers (USA) 2013-Present

Individual Accolades

  • MLS Cup MVP: 2015
  • MLS MVP: 2017
  • MLS Best XI: 2013, 2014, 2017
  • MLS All-Star: 2014, 2016-18
  • MLS Newcomer of the Year: 2013
